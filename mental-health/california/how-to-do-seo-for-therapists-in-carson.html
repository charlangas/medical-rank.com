10 Ways to Make Your Google Business Profile Shine

  1. Claim Your Spot: Stake your claim on that Google Business Profile. It's free real estate!
  2. Leave No Stone Unturned: Fill out every section. You never know which detail might resonate with a future patient.
  3. Choose Your Categories Wisely: Pick the most accurate primary category for your mental health practice.
  4. Visual Appeal: Upload professional images of your practice, staff, and facilities. (Save those beach photos for your personal social media.)
  5. Stay Active: Regularly refresh your profile with new posts and practice news.
  6. Embrace Feedback: Solicit reviews and address each one. That's right, every single one, including the not-so-happy campers.
  7. Q&A Like a Boss: Answer questions before they're even asked. Be a mind reader!
  8. Keep It Fresh: Ensure all details are current. Your practice evolves, and so should your profile.
  9. Message Me Maybe: Enable messaging if it's available in your area. Be there when patients need you.
  10. Data Nerd Alert: Use Google's Insights to see how you're doing and tweak your strategy.
