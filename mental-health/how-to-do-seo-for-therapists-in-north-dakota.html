8 Strategies to Transform Your Website into a patient Magnet

  1. Target Local Keywords: Think like your patients. What are they typing into Google at 2 AM?
    • "Top-rated Therapist North Dakota"
    • "24/7 mental health services North Dakota"
    • "Highly recommended Therapist North Dakota"
  2. Build Location-Specific Pages: Serve multiple areas? Create pages for each one. Show some love to all your North Dakota neighborhoods!
  3. Improve Your 'About Us' and 'Contact' Pages: Ensure your business details are uniform across all platforms. Adding a Google Map is like leaving breadcrumbs for patient.
  4. Show Some Local Love: Write about health issues relevant to North Dakota. Engage with community initiatives. Highlight your local mental health heroes.
  5. Reviews Are Everything: Encourage happy patients to spread the love online. And always respond - good or bad. (Yes, even to that one patient who complains about everything.)
  6. Build Local Street Credibility: Secure spots in local business listings and mental health registries. Partner with North Dakota enterprises. Aim to be the most recognized Therapist in town!
  7. Tighten Your Loose Bolts: Make sure your site is mobile-friendly, speedy, and easy for search engines to understand. No one likes a slow, clunky website - especially not Google.
  8. Blog Like a Local: Create a blog addressing North Dakota-specific mental health concerns. Become the trusted voice for local mental health matters.

10 Ways to Make Your Google Business Profile Shine

  1. Secure Your Profile: Take ownership of your Google Business Profile. It's like planting your flag in the digital landscape!
  2. Leave No Stone Unturned: Fill out every section. You never know which detail might resonate with a future patient.
  3. Select Categories Carefully: Opt for the category that best represents your Mental Health Practice.
  4. Visual Appeal: Upload professional images of your practice, staff, and facilities. (Save those beach photos for your personal social media.)
  5. Stay Active: Regularly refresh your profile with new posts and practice news.
  6. Review Central: Encourage reviews and respond to all of them. Yes, all of them. Even the grumpy ones.
  7. Anticipate Inquiries: Provide answers to potential questions proactively. Channel your inner psychic!
  8. Maintain Accuracy: Regularly review and update your information. Change is the only constant, after all.
  9. Open Communication: Turn on messaging features if offered in your region. Make yourself accessible to patient.
  10. Analyze and Adapt: Leverage Google's Insights to track performance and refine your approach.
