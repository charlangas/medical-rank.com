10 Tactics for a Standout Google Business Profile

  1. Claim Your Spot: Stake your claim on that Google Business Profile. It's free real estate!
  2. Complete Every Detail: Populate all available fields. Each piece of information could be the deciding factor for a potential client.
  3. Select Categories Carefully: Opt for the category that best represents your Med Spa.
  4. Showcase Your Space: Include top-notch pictures of your clinic, team, and technology. (This isn't the place for your holiday snapshots.)
  5. Keep It Current: Maintain an active profile by sharing updates and important information frequently.
  6. Manage Your Reputation: Prompt patients for reviews and respond to each. Yes, that means all of them, even the Negative Nancys.
  7. Q&A Like a Boss: Answer questions before they're even asked. Be a mind reader!
  8. Maintain Accuracy: Regularly review and update your information. Change is the only constant, after all.
  9. Open Communication: Turn on messaging features if offered in your region. Make yourself accessible to client.
  10. Numbers Don't Lie: Utilize Google's Insights to measure success and adjust your tactics accordingly.
