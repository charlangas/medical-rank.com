10 Tactics for a Standout Google Business Profile

  1. Claim Your Spot: Stake your claim on that Google Business Profile. It's free real estate!
  2. Leave No Stone Unturned: Fill out every section. You never know which detail might resonate with a future client.
  3. Select Categories Carefully: Opt for the category that best represents your Med Spa.
  4. Picture Perfect: Add high-quality photos of your office, team, and equipment. (No, your vacation selfies don't count.)
  5. Post It Up: Keep your profile fresh with regular updates and announcements.
  6. Manage Your Reputation: Prompt patients for reviews and respond to each. Yes, that means all of them, even the Negative Nancys.
  7. Preemptive Problem Solving: Address common queries before they're asked. Show off those Med Spa prediction skills!
  8. Keep It Fresh: Ensure all details are current. Your practice evolves, and so should your profile.
  9. Message Me Maybe: Enable messaging if it's available in your area. Be there when patients need you.
  10. Analyze and Adapt: Leverage Google's Insights to track performance and refine your approach.
