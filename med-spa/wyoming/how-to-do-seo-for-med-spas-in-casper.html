10 Tactics for a Standout Google Business Profile

  3. Be Precise with Categories: Choose the primary category that truly reflects your med spa specialization.
  4. Visual Appeal: Upload professional images of your practice, staff, and facilities. (Save those beach photos for your personal social media.)

  6. Review Central: Encourage reviews and respond to all of them. Yes, all of them. Even the grumpy ones.
