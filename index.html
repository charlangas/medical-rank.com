What if I've already tried SEO and it didn't work for my practice?

10 out of 10 times this means that you hired the wrong person to do SEO, or you were trying to do it yourself. SEO works. Whoever is showing up at the top of Google when you search for a service is investing in SEO. It doesn't mean that building a sales channel centered on SEO will be quick or easy. But it absolutely works when it's done right.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

While some improvements can be seen within a few weeks, significant results typically take 3-6 months. SEO is a long-term strategy that builds momentum over time.

Do you guarantee first page rankings?

We don't guarantee specific rankings as search engines constantly evolve. However, we do guarantee to implement best practices and strategies that have consistently improved our clients' visibility and traffic.

How do you measure the success of your SEO campaigns?

We track various metrics including organic traffic growth, keyword rankings, conversion rates, and ultimately, the increase in new patient inquiries or appointments booked through your website.
