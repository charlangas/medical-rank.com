8 Ways to Make Your Website a Patient-Attracting Machine

  1. Target Local Keywords: Think like your patients. What are they typing into Google at 2 AM?
    • "Chiropractor in Knoxville"
    • "Urgent chiropractic help Knoxville"
    • "Highly recommended Chiropractor Knoxville"
  2. Create Location-Specific Pages: Serve multiple areas? Create pages for each one. Show some love to all your Knoxville neighborhoods!
  3. Improve Your 'About Us' and 'Contact' Pages: Maintain consistency in your contact information. A Google Map on your page is like a virtual "You Are Here" sign.
  4. Show Some Local Love: Write about health issues relevant to Knoxville. Engage with community initiatives. Highlight your local chiropractic heroes.
  5. Reviews Are Gold: Encourage happy patients to spread the love online. And always respond - good or bad. (Yes, even to that one patient who complains about everything.)
  6. Build Local Street Credibility: Ensure your practice appears in Knoxville directories and chiropractic listings. Collaborate with nearby establishments. Become Knoxville's go-to Chiropractor!
  7. Tighten Your Loose Bolts: Optimize your site for mobile devices, quick loading times, and search engine crawlability. Remember, a sluggish site is the fastest way to lose visitors and search rankings.
  8. Blog Like a Local: Create a blog addressing Knoxville-specific chiropractic concerns. Become the trusted voice for local chiropractic matters.

For many patient in Knoxville, this is their initial encounter with your practice. Time to make it shine!

10 Ways to Make Your Google Business Profile Shine

  1. Claim Your Spot: Stake your claim on that Google Business Profile. It's free real estate!
  2. Fill It All In: Don't leave any field empty. Every bit of info helps patients choose you.
  3. Choose Your Categories Wisely: Pick the most accurate primary category for your chiropractic practice.
  4. Showcase Your Space: Include top-notch pictures of your clinic, team, and technology. (This isn't the place for your holiday snapshots.)
  5. Stay Active: Regularly refresh your profile with new posts and practice news.
  6. Review Central: Encourage reviews and respond to all of them. Yes, all of them. Even the grumpy ones.
  7. Q&A Like a Boss: Answer questions before they're even asked. Be a mind reader!
  8. Keep It Fresh: Ensure all details are current. Your practice evolves, and so should your profile.
  9. Message Me Maybe: Enable messaging if it's available in your area. Be there when patients need you.
  10. Data Nerd Alert: Use Google's Insights to see how you're doing and tweak your strategy.
