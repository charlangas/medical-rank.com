8 Tactics to Optimize Your Website for patient Attraction

  1. Target Local Keywords: Put yourself in your patient's shoes. What would they search for in the middle of the night?
    • "Chiropractor in Philadelphia"
    • "Urgent chiropractic help Philadelphia"
    • "Best Chiropractor in Philadelphia"
  2. Create Location-Specific Pages: Serve multiple areas? Create pages for each one. Show some love to all your Philadelphia neighborhoods!
  3. Pimp Your 'About Us' and 'Contact' Pages: Maintain consistency in your contact information. A Google Map on your page is like a virtual "You Are Here" sign.
  4. Show Some Local Love: Write about health issues relevant to Philadelphia. Engage with community initiatives. Highlight your local chiropractic heroes.
  5. Reviews Are Everything: Motivate satisfied patient to share their experiences. Respond to all feedback, positive or negative. (Even the chronic complainers deserve attention.)
  6. Build Local Street Cred: Secure spots in local business listings and chiropractic registries. Partner with Philadelphia enterprises. Aim to be the most recognized Chiropractor in town!
  7. Tighten Your Loose Bolts: Ensure your website is responsive, fast-loading, and search engine-friendly. Slow, complicated sites are a turn-off for both patient and Google.
  8. Blog Like a Local: Launch a blog centered around Philadelphia's chiropractic scene. Establish yourself as the local chiropractic authority.

For many patient in Philadelphia, this is their initial encounter with your practice. Time to make it shine!

10 Strategies to Elevate Your Google Business Profile

  1. Establish Your Presence: Grab that Google Business Profile. It's your complimentary plot in the online world!
  2. Complete Every Detail: Populate all available fields. Each piece of information could be the deciding factor for a potential patient.
  3. Choose Your Categories Wisely: Pick the most accurate primary category for your chiropractic practice.
  4. Showcase Your Space: Include top-notch pictures of your clinic, team, and technology. (This isn't the place for your holiday snapshots.)
  5. Post It Up: Keep your profile fresh with regular updates and announcements.
  6. Embrace Feedback: Solicit reviews and address each one. That's right, every single one, including the not-so-happy campers.
  7. Anticipate Inquiries: Provide answers to potential questions proactively. Channel your inner psychic!
  8. Keep It Fresh: Ensure all details are current. Your practice evolves, and so should your profile.
  9. Open Communication: Turn on messaging features if offered in your region. Make yourself accessible to patient.
  10. Numbers Don't Lie: Utilize Google's Insights to measure success and adjust your tactics accordingly.
