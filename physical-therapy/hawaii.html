The unique aspects of practicing in Hawaii extend beyond the physical environment. The healthy, active lifestyle of many Hawaiians can also shape the physiotherapist's role. From helping local surfers recover from injuries to working with elderly patients to maintain their mobility and independence, the physiotherapist in Hawaii must be adaptable and willing to tailor their services to the unique needs of the community.
