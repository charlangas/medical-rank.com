  3. Improve Your 'About Us' and 'Contact' Pages: Ensure your business details are uniform across all platforms. Adding a Google Map is like leaving breadcrumbs for patient.
  4. Show Some Local Flavor: Create content focused on Providence's health scene. Participate in local happenings. Showcase your Providence-based team.

10 Ways to Make Your Google Business Profile Shine

  1. Claim Your Spot: Stake your claim on that Google Business Profile. It's free real estate!
  2. Fill It All In: Don't leave any field empty. Every bit of info helps patients choose you.
  3. Select Categories Carefully: Opt for the category that best represents your Medical Practice.
  4. Picture Perfect: Add high-quality photos of your office, team, and equipment. (No, your vacation selfies don't count.)
  5. Stay Active: Regularly refresh your profile with new posts and practice news.
  6. Review Central: Encourage reviews and respond to all of them. Yes, all of them. Even the grumpy ones.
  7. Anticipate Inquiries: Provide answers to potential questions proactively. Channel your inner psychic!
  8. Stay Current: Keep your info up-to-date. Things change, right?
  9. Stay Connected: Activate messaging options if they're available. Be ready to engage when patient reach out.
  10. Analyze and Adapt: Leverage Google's Insights to track performance and refine your approach.
