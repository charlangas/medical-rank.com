  1. Establish Your Presence: Grab that Google Business Profile. It's your complimentary plot in the online world!

  3. Choose Your Categories Wisely: Pick the most accurate primary category for your medical practice.
  4. Picture Perfect: Add high-quality photos of your office, team, and equipment. (No, your vacation selfies don't count.)
  5. Stay Active: Regularly refresh your profile with new posts and practice news.
  6. Manage Your Reputation: Prompt patients for reviews and respond to each. Yes, that means all of them, even the Negative Nancys.
