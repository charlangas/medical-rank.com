8 Ways to Make Your Website a Patient-Attracting Machine

  1. Target Local Keywords: Put yourself in your client's shoes. What would they search for in the middle of the night?
    • "Nutritionist in Orlando"
    • "Urgent nutrition help Orlando"
    • "Orlando's favorite Nutritionist"
  2. Build Location-Specific Pages: Serve multiple areas? Create pages for each one. Show some love to all your Orlando neighborhoods!
  3. Improve Your 'About Us' and 'Contact' Pages: Maintain consistency in your contact information. A Google Map on your page is like a virtual "You Are Here" sign.
  4. Show Some Local Flavor: Blog about Orlando health topics. Get involved in community events. Spotlight your awesome local staff.
  5. Reviews Are Everything: Inspire your pleased client to leave reviews. Address all comments, the good and the bad. (Yes, including that one perpetually unsatisfied individual.)
  6. Build Local Street Credibility: Ensure your practice appears in Orlando directories and nutrition listings. Collaborate with nearby establishments. Become Orlando's go-to Nutritionist!
  7. Tech It Up: Make sure your site is mobile-friendly, speedy, and easy for search engines to understand. No one likes a slow, clunky website - especially not Google.
  8. Blog Like a Local: Launch a blog centered around Orlando's nutrition scene. Establish yourself as the local nutrition authority.

For many client in Orlando, this is their initial encounter with your practice. Time to make it shine!

10 Strategies to Elevate Your Google Business Profile

  1. Claim Your Spot: Stake your claim on that Google Business Profile. It's free real estate!
  2. Complete Every Detail: Populate all available fields. Each piece of information could be the deciding factor for a potential client.
  3. Be Precise with Categories: Choose the primary category that truly reflects your nutrition specialization.
  4. Showcase Your Space: Include top-notch pictures of your clinic, team, and technology. (This isn't the place for your holiday snapshots.)
  5. Stay Active: Regularly refresh your profile with new posts and practice news.
  6. Review Central: Encourage reviews and respond to all of them. Yes, all of them. Even the grumpy ones.
  7. Q&A Like a Boss: Answer questions before they're even asked. Be a mind reader!
  8. Keep It Fresh: Ensure all details are current. Your practice evolves, and so should your profile.
  9. Message Me Maybe: Enable messaging if it's available in your area. Be there when patients need you.
  10. Data Nerd Alert: Use Google's Insights to see how you're doing and tweak your strategy.
